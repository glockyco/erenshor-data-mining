To defeat the Warded Shadow, first defeat the two Phantom's Ward on either side of it.  While attacking the Phantom's Ward, one of the purple blobs that spawn will have particles above it, defeat those to continue attacking the Phantom's Wards.  Once both are down, the Warded Shadow can be defeated.
