''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} roams [[The Blight]] and can be encountered throughout the area.

=Appearance=
The {{PAGENAME}} is a deep purple dragon with a black carapace and large horns.

=Behavior=
Exploring The Blight can be extremely dangerous, as {{PAGENAME}} can be found roaming throughout most of the zone. It has incredibly acute senses, and will zero in on adventurers at quite a large distance. Because it can be quite difficult to spot due to the area's thick haze, players should instead listen for the idle sound cues that signal {{PAGENAME}} is approaching.

===Spells & Abilities===
On-Hit:
*'''Blight Shock''' (15% chance to stun all targets in the immediate vicinity)

===Strategy===
The {{PAGENAME}} is one of the most difficult enemies in the game, and should not be fought during the player's first venture into The Blight.

Because [[Arcanist|Arcanists]] have a low health pool and might easily be one-shot if they draw aggro, it's a good idea to bring a second [[Paladin]] to the fight. Double [[Status Effects|Debuffs]] such as {{AbilityLink|Glare of Soluna}} and {{AbilityLink|Theft of Vigor|image=spectrum.png}} can also greatly decrease the {{PAGENAME}}'s damage. Positioning is also greatly important to avoid {{PAGENAME}}'s on-hit stun; the party's healer and damage dealers should avoid standing near the tank.

=Notes=

The Blight Wyrm can be killed with {{ItemLink|Wyrm's Bane}} if you are low level and dying to it, though this will prevent you from getting loot and experience.
