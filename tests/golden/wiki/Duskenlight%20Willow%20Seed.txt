Needed for the quest [[Willow Seeds]]. It is located at coordinates X: 1151.8 x Y: 63.4 x Z: 774.12 behind a hill in [[Duskenlight Coast]]. This is the hardest seed to find. To get to it, head north along the road past the giant willow tree and follow the hill around to the left. There is a small green hill you can climb. Follow it around behind the larger hill, above the coast, and you will find the seed on a ledge.
