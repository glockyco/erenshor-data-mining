'''{{main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
There is an '''80% chance''' for {{PAGENAME}} to replace the [[A Lost Sea Giant|Lost Sea Giant]] on the far eastern cape of [[Vitheo's Watch]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} uses the same sprite as the male variants of [[A Lost Sea Giant|Lost Sea Giants]], but is slightly larger. He is a blue, semi-transparent apparition wearing a helmet, armor, and gloves. He can also occasionally be spotted wielding the [[Sea Giant's Cutlass]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the ruins on the coast. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or a nearby Lost Sea Giant are provoked.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} should not be much of a threat alone, but be careful not to get his attention too early while clearing out the regular mobs around him.
