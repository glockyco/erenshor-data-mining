Occuphage, Eater of Eyes is an NPC located inside the Lighthouse on the [[Docks|Docks (Stowaway's Step)]]. After entering using the [[Stowaway Step Lighthouse Key]] you should walk down the steps to the drop-off point and fall down, here you will be transitioned into a secret section of the [[Island Tomb]] beside Occuphage. 

It can also be handed [[Eye of Plaxitheris]] for the repeatable quest {{QuestLink | questlink=Seaslimed Bracelet (Quest){{!}}Seaslimed Bracelet}}
