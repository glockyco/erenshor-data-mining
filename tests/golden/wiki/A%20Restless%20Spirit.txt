'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found throughout the [[Island Tomb]] on [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a floating green ghost. It is semi-transparent and wears an ethereal helmet on what appears to be a skeletal head.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the halls of the tomb and will attack players if they get too close. They can often be found around corners, waiting to ambush unsuspecting adventurers.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
