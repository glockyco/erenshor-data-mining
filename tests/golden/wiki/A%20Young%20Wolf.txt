'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} are common throughout southern Erenshor and can be found in [[Hidden Hills]], [[Vitheo's Watch]], [[Faerie's Brake]], [[Willowwatch Ridge]] and on [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a small pup with grey and white fur.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a player to attack. Being adolescents, they are quite weak.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

=Notes=
*There is a small chance for {{PAGENAME}} to be replaced by an [[Alpha Wolf]] in both Hidden Hills and Faerie's Brake.
