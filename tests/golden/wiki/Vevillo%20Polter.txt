''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found at the end of the Bandit's Lair on [[Stowaway's Step]]. The cave entrance can be found near [[Treven Pines|Treven Pines']] shop.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a bandit wearing grey and red clothes, shoes, and gloves. He has brown hair, a brown goatee, and can occasionally be seen holding a [[Brackwood Mace]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits behind a table at the back of the lair. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he is attacked or [[Lunar Madness|provoked]].

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} will likely be one of the first bosses players encounter. As such, he does not have any particularly noteworthy skills. If players are struggling to defeat him, they may want to form a [[Simulated Players|party]] or consider gaining a few levels through weaker enemies or by completing [[Quests]] in Stowaway's Step.
