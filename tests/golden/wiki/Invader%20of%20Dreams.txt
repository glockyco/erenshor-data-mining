''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found at the end of the second [[Mysterious Portal]] in [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]]. Accessing the portal requires completing {{QuestLink|The Fernallan Ritual}}.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large ethereal demon with glowing red eyes. It is semi-transparent, and has two large wings growing out of its back.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits at the end of the path in the second Mysterious Portal, waiting for anyone brave enough to invade its realm. It will attack if players get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} will summon an orb of [[Malignant Thoughts]] upon reaching 66% health, which continuously spawns in [[Nightmarian Knight|Nightmarian Knights]], [[Nightmarian Arcanist|Nightmarian Arcanists]], and [[Nightmarian Hound|Nightmarian Hounds]] until it is defeated. This portal should be targeted and destroyed as quickly as possible before the party gets swarmed by additional enemies.<br>

At 33% health, two more Malignant Thoughts are formed. Just like before, these should immediately be taken out to avoid too many additional enemies entering the fight.

=Notes=
*{{PAGENAME}} is an instanced boss, meaning you can leave the zone and he will immediately respawn.
