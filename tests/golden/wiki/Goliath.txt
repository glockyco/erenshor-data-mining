'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[Enemies#List of Bosses|Boss]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found in [[Blacksalt Strand]], patrolling the mountains between the entrances to the [[Braxonian Desert]] and [[Elderstone Mines]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large ape. It is a much bigger version of [[A Seaside Gorilla|Seaside Gorilla]] with brown fur and orange eyes.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the mountains and will attack players or NPCs in its vicinity.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
Players wandering into Blacksalt Strand for the first time may be underleveled for a fight with {{PAGENAME}}, making him a dangerous foe to encounter due to his durability and high attack strength. Because he will aggro onto nearby NPCs, players can get the nearby [[Azure Guard]] to assist in the battle by dragging the boss near him. He is one of two bosses that drop the level 3 class-specific [[Auras|Aura]] items, making him well worth farming.<ref>[[Gruhglor]] can also drop these items, though at a slightly lower rate.</ref>
