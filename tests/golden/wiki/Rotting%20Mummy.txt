''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is a rare variant of [[A Sorrowful Spirit]] and can occasionally replace them in the eastern part of the [[Island Tomb]] on [[Stowaway's Step]], near where [[Failed Cremation]] spawns.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a corpse covered in bandages.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} stand in the dark corridors of the tomb, waiting to ambush unsuspecting adventurers. While they are unable to use any spells, their physical attacks are relatively strong compared to other enemies in the dungeon.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
