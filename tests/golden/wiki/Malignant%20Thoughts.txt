''{{Main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} are unique to the second [[Mysterious Portal]]. One is found on the path through the dungeon, while three more are summoned during the fight with the [[Invader of Dreams]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a floating purple orb.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} are stationary and do not attack the player, though they can and should be attacked and destroyed themselves. They will summon either a [[Nightmarian Knight]], [[Nightmarian Arcanist]], or [[Nightmarian Hound]] five times in quick succession, as well as two more times at much longer interval. After seven enemies have been summoned, {{PAGENAME}} becomes inactive.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

=Notes=
*Although they will eventually stop summoning new enemies, it's still a good idea to take out {{PAGENAME}} as quickly as possible to avoid the party being overwhelmed.
