''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found throughout the central caves of the [[Elderstone Mines]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a serpentine enemy wearing bangles on each wrist and a belt. As the name implies, its body is a deep green. It has sharp claws and a forked tongue.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the mines and will attack players if they get too close. They are physically weaker than the [[A Deepling Crimsonite|Deepling Crimsonites]], but make up for this by casting a small variety of spells.
