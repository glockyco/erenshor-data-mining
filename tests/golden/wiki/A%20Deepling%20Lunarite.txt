''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found throughout the central caves of the [[Elderstone Mines]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a serpentine enemy wearing bangles on each wrist and a belt. Its scales are black, while its body is orange. It has sharp claws and a forked tongue.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the caves and will attack players if they get too close. Physically, it is the weakest of the three Deeplings, but what it lacks it might it makes up for with offensive spells.
