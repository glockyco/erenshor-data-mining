'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found exclusively on the path up from the [[Docks]] to the rest of [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a skeleton wearing tattered rags. He has manacles attached to his wrists, which possibly explains how he earned his nickname.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} stands on the path leading to [[Treven Pines|Treven Pines']] shop.<ref>There is a chance {{PAGENAME}} will already have been defeated by a [[Simulated Players|Simulated Player]] when the player discovers it, though its corpse will still be available for looting should this occur.</ref> It will attack players when they approach it. {{PAGENAME}} is likely the second [[Enemies|enemy]] players will encounter, and the second part of the combat tutorial. Upon moving up the hill towards {{PAGENAME}}, a tutorial window will pop up encouraging players to make sure they've learned their starting [[Ability Books|abilities]]:

{| class="wikitable" style=width:38em
|+Spells and Skills
|align="center" |
If there are [[Ability Books|spell books or skill books]] in your [[inventory]], <span style="color: #01d900;">RIGHT CLICK on them to consume them and learn their abilities.</span> Then open up your SPELL BOOK and SKILL BOOK with <span style="color: #01d900;">'B'</span> to make hotkeys for abilities that you want to use.
|}

After defeating {{PAGENAME}} and moving up the hill, another tutorial pop-up will also teach players about looting:

{| class="wikitable" style=width:38em
|+Get Paid!

When you win a battle, make sure you <span style="color: #01d900;">RIGHT CLICK</span> on the fallen enemy to take his loot!
|}
