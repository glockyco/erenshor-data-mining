This Mold can be found in [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]] by fishing, it can be fished at any time of the day. To craft the normal {{ItemLink|Ceremonial Ring}} you need to use {{ItemLink|Coal}} as a fuel source, but if you want to craft the {{ItemLink|Pristine Ceremonial Ring}} you need to use {{ItemLink|Ancient Coal}} as a fuel source.
