''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} appears in the central crater of [[Soluna's Landing]] after all nine [[Stardust|Stardusts]] have been defeated.

=Appearance=
Astra is a giant blue dragon with red eyes and large black horns.

=Behavior=
Astra appears in a brilliant flash of white light after the last Stardust has been destroyed, and will attack players if they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
On-Hit:
*{{AbilityLink|Brax's Fury}} ('''20%''' chance)
Offensive:
*'''Cosmic Breath''' (Base '''2,000''' damage AoE)

===Strategy===
Summoning {{PAGENAME}} requires players to give each of the nine Stardusts scattered throughout the crater a {{ItemLink|Lost Faith}} to make them vulnerable, and then defeat them. Once the last Stardust has fallen, Astra will immediately spawn in the center of the crater.

Astra is one of the most difficult enemies in the game; players should be at or around level 35 and have several pieces of [[Item Quality|Blessed]] end-game gear before attempting to face her. Resistances are especially important, as Cosmic Breath's damage can be greatly lowered by stacking Magic and Elemental resistances.

Because [[Arcanist|Arcanists]] have a low health pool and might easily be one-shot by Astra, one strategy is to bring a second [[Paladin]] to the fight. Double [[Status Effects|Debuffs]] such as {{AbilityLink|Glare of Soluna}} and {{AbilityLink|Theft of Vigor|image=spectrum.png}} can greatly decrease Astra's damage.

=Notes=
*Defeating {{PAGENAME}} is required to complete {{QuestLink|Raval's Last Wish}}.

*If {{PAGENAME}} is respawned via a {{ItemLink|Time Stone}}, all [[Stardust]] will respawn as well and must be destroyed again.
