Aragath is found heading into Plax's area but hugging left and going down the slope instead of going over to Plax. Aragath only appears at night time, 11PM to be exact. He is the questgiver for {{QuestLink|link=Aragath's Imprisonment}} and the target for {{QuestLink|link=Destroying Aragath}}
