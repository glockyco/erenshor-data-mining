''{{Main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}}s can be found exclusively on the beaches of [[Duskenlight Coast]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}}s are blue spheres of water. They are the elemental embodiment of both air and sea, given form.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}}s bounce across the sand until sensing a target to attack. Alone they do not pose much of a threat, but their close proximity to each other means that players may end up fighting multiple at once if they aren't careful.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
