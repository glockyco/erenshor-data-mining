''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is located in the [[Lost Cellar]]. After entering the dungeon, go straight and take a right at the fork. {{PAGENAME}} can be found in the dead end only slightly further down the path.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large insect with a blue body and purple legs. It has two large horns and mandibles, as well as a brown exoskeleton running along its back. It is guarded by two [[Spark Soldier|Spark Soldiers]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in a dead end towards the beginning of the cave. It will attack players if they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
Although {{PAGENAME}} should not pose much of a threat to an adequately leveled and geared party, combined with its guards a [[Paladin]] might find themselves overwhelmed. {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}} or {{AbilityLink|Sunburst}} can momentarily take them out of the fight while they are killed or the party focuses on taking out the {{PAGENAME}}.

=Notes=
*Although its stats are primarily geared towards magic users, [[Royal Carapace]] is an excellent and reliable early upgrade for shield-using Paladins.
*Because of its close proximity to the [[Spark Queen]], both bosses can quickly and easily be farmed simultaneously.
