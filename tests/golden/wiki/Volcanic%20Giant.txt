''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found in the central crater of the volcano in [[Rockshade Hold]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is an orange ethereal giant wearing a helmet and armor.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the volcano's crater and will attack players if they get too close. His detection radius is incredibly small, however, which means players will likely need to engage {{PAGENAME}} in order to draw him out of the crater.

===Spells & Abilities===
Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Brax's Rage}}

===Strategy===
Fighting {{PAGENAME}} in the volcano's crater causes constant ticks of '''175''' damage from the heat. Players can pull {{PAGENAME}} out of the crater to avoid this damage if they wish, although it should not be enough damage to matter too much at the level {{PAGENAME}} should be fought.
