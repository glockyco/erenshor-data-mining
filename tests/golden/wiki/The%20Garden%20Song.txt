Each of the bells near the garden has a word printed on it. Using the lyrics in the song, click the bells to ring them in the correct order:
*Vision
*Passion
*Luck
*Flourish
*Passion
*Vision
*Luck
The order of the bells (with their names visible) from left to right, is: 2 → 3 → 4 → 1 → 3 → 2 → 4.
