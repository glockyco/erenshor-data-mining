''{{Main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is a '''rare''' enemy with a '''1% chance''' to replace [[A Brown Bear Cub|Brown Bear Cubs]], [[A Brown Bear|Brown Bears]], [[Spectral Bear|Spectral Bears]], and [[Dryad Spirit|Dryad Spirits]] in the southern section of [[Duskenlight Coast]], with the exception of those in the eastern corner where [[Massia the Fallen]] spawns. There are 20 possible spawn points for the {{PAGENAME}}, for a total '''18.2% chance''' to spawn him when entering the [[Zones|Zone]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large demi-human creature with light blue skin and a white beard. He wears a sash across his chest, a horned helmet on his head, and a green and brown kilt. As the name implies, {{PAGENAME}} is massive and can easily be spotted from afar when entering the zone.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}}s roam the landscape until sensing a target to attack. Not only are they aggressive to players, but they will also attack other enemies in their immediate vicinity.

===Spells & Abilities===
Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Tangle}}

===Strategy===
Finding {{PAGENAME}} is half the battle, and may require players clearing out low-level spawns multiple times, or else repeatedly reloading the zone in an attempt to force him to spawn. When fighting {{PAGENAME}}, make sure to stay close to him; he can use [[Tangle]] to lock a target in place for several seconds, which may end up keeping a member of your party out of attack range.

=Notes=
*It is possible for multiple {{PAGENAME}}s to be present at the same time.
