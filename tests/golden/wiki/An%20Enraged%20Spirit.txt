'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found throughout the [[Island Tomb]] on [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a floating red ghost resembling a woman. It is semi-transparent and wears an ethereal flowing dress.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} stand at various points in the tomb and will attack players if they get too close. They can often be found around corners and behind doors, waiting to ambush unsuspecting adventurers.
