=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a white-haired necromancer and follower and apparent member of [[The Children of Sivakaya]]. He wears a brown robe and can occasionally be seen holding an [[Idol of Sivakaya]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits near a throne at the back of the tomb. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he or a nearby [[A Sorrowful Spirit|Sorrowful Spirits]] are attacked or [[Lunar Madness|provoked]].

===Spells & Abilities===
Buffs:
*{{AbilityLink|Minor Protection}}
Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Brax's Touch}}
*{{AbilityLink|Weak Poison}}

===Strategy===
With careful positioning, it is possible to attract the attention of the nearby Sorrowful Spirits so that they can be killed before attempting to fight {{PAGENAME}}. Although he is not particularly strong, his spells can still do a modest amount of damage. A [[Paladin]] or [[Duelist]] can interrupt {{PAGENAME}} while he is casting with a well-timed {{AbilityLink|Kick}}.
