'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[Enemies#List of Bosses|Boss]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found on a plateau in northern [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]] near the entrance to [[Rottenfoot]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large golem made of brown and grey stones. He is also covered in several wreaths of vines and moss.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the plateau above the encampment of [[A Rottenfoot Warrior|Rottenfoot Warriors]] and [[Rottenfoot Swampwalker|Rottenfoot Swampwalkers]] and will attack players if they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} is likely one of the first bosses players will encounter with a summoning mechanic: when he reaches 60% and 20% health, he will spawn two [[A Rock Minion|Rock Minions]] nearby. These additional enemies should be taken out or stunned quickly, as they will reduce the ability of the party's [[Paladin]] to effectively tank.

{{PAGENAME}} is one of two bosses that drop the level 3 class-specific [[Auras|Aura]] items, making him well worth farming.<ref>[[Goliath]] can also drop these items at a slightly higher rate, but is a higher level and more difficult fight.</ref>
