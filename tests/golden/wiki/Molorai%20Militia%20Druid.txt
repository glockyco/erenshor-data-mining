''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found throughout [[Old Krakengard]] and outside the dungeon in [[Vitheo's Watch]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}}s are spellcasters wearing silver and yellow clothes, gloves, and boots. They can occasionally be seen wielding a [[Pathfinder's Staff]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} stand guard and patrol various points in and outside of Old Krakengard. They will attack players if they get too close, or if they or other nearby militia members are [[Lunar Madness|provoked]]. {{PAGENAME}}s keep themselves [[Status Effects|buffed]] when idle, but will only cast offensive spells when in combat.
