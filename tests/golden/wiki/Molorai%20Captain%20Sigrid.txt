=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a bearded man in black armor with a red scar under his right eye. He is flanked by two [[Molorai Attack Dog|Molorai Attack Dogs]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in his room at the back of the bunk area of the fort. Although he will not attack as soon as players open the door, he will become aggressive if players get too close or [[Lunar Madness|Provoke]] him or his dogs.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} should not pose much of a threat for players who have managed to make it through the fort to him. To make the fight even easier, his dogs can be quickly dispatched once the fight begins or stunned using {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}}.
