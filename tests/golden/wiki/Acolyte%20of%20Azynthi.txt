Once [[Eldoth Molorai (Character)|Eldoth Molorai]] has been defeated, type in chat:
<pre>
/shout Azynthi
</pre>
whilst in [[Azynthi's Garden]]. This will permanently unlock the ability to summon {{PAGENAME}}.

He can then be fought by simply typing <span style="color: #15e300;">[Azynthi]</span> while next to [[A Strange Hermit]].

The Acolyte of Azynthi does constant AoE damage and summons [[Greater Azynthian Icebone]] and [[Greater Azynthian Firebone]] in pairs during combat.
