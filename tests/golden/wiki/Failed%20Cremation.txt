=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a skeleton, likely reanimated by the [[Woe|necromancers]] in the tomb who failed to control him.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in a back room of the tomb. He has no detection range, meaning players will not attract his attention unless they initiate the fight or get immediately next to him.

===Spells & Abilities===
Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Theft of Life}}

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} is the second most difficult boss in the tomb, though he still does not pose much of a challenge. His AC is relatively high compared to most other enemies in the dungeon, making him slightly more difficult to hit. Additionally, Theft of Life can heal back some of {{PAGENAME}}'s health, though a [[Paladin]] or [[Duelist]] can interrupt {{PAGENAME}} while he is casting with a well-timed {{AbilityLink|Kick}}.
