''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} has a '''33% chance''' to replace one of the [[A Faerie Drone|Faerie Drones]] near the ruins in the northeast of [[Faerie's Brake]]. Because the Faerie Drones move about and it can be difficult to tell which one shares a spawn table with {{PAGENAME}}, it is recommended for players to either clear out all of the Faerie Drones or continually re-enter the [[Zones|Zone]] from [[Port Azure]] when searching for her.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a faerie with blue wings and hair. She wears a pink top and pink and white skirt. {{PAGENAME}} is several times larger than the other nearby Faerie Drones, making her easy to spot from a distance.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams amongst the rest of the faeries. She will attack players if they get too close, or if she or a nearby Faerie Drone are [[Lunar Madness|provoked]].

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} is not particularly strong, though she does boast several spells that, when combined, can add up to a significant amount of damage on low-HP targets. A [[Paladin]] or [[Duelist]] can interrupt {{PAGENAME}} while she is casting these abilities with a well-timed {{AbilityLink|Kick}}.
