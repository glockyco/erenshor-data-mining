=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} has a '''25% chance''' to replace [[A Deepling Brute]] along the path to [[Ravager, The First Infected]] in the [[Elderstone Mines]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is dark green with red and pink pustules dotting its body. It also has several large gashes along its body, seemingly stitched together with roots.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} stands guard in the caves and will attack players if they get too close. It is physically slightly stronger than the Deepling Brutes it replaces, but is otherwise rather unremarkable.
