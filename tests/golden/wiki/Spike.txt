''{{Main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} has a '''50% chance''' to replace the center [[A Nagalok|Nagalok]] against the giant rune at the back of the Nagalok Den in northeast [[Duskenlight Coast]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large purple Nagalok with massive blue spikes protruding from its back, head, and arms.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in the back of the cave. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or the nearby Nagaloks are attacked or provoked.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} is much more durable and hits much harder than the other Nagaloks in the cave. Players can either take out the two Nagaloks near him quickly, or stun them using {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}} or {{AbilityLink|Sunburst}} to help the party's tank more effectively block {{PAGENAME}}'s attacks.

=Notes=
*[[Helm of the Deep]] is an excellent mid-game [[Armor|Helmet]] for a tank [[Paladin]], making {{PAGENAME}} quite worth seeking out.
