Mephallo can be found on the ghostly bridge located in the [[Shivering Step]].

To make the bridge appear you must first collect the [[Guide to Sight]] through fishing in [[Shivering Step]].

After you inspect the book head to the large gargoyle statue or where the Vitheo statue is on [[Stowaway's Step|Stowaways Steps]]. 

A large ghostly bridge should now be there where you can cross and battle Mephallo.
