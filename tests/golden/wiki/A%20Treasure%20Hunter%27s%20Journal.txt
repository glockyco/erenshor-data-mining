Benjamin's Journal

Entry 1:

I have caught wind of the ultimate prize - a blessing more than a treasure even!

It holds secrets that no mortal should have any right to know, it holds the location of a place that can bestow the touch of a God upon any ordinary item!

A tribute is needed, or so I would guess, but the tribute is the least of my concerns. I am after the instructions. 

Entry 2:

It would seem that the pieces I need are on this very land. There more proof to justify my search than there is to condemn it. I shall meander with purpose, I think.

Entry 3:

I have been through many of the chasms in this place. Through moldy dungeons and man-made pits of filth and for my efforts I have found nothing.

I have narrowed my area of interest down however - a place called Abyssal Lake holds great interest, though I know not who dwells within. If that fails, I believe the skyfolk may have my answer.
