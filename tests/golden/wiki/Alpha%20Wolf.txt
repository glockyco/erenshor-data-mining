''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is a '''rare''' enemy, replacing [[A Young Wolf]] in [[Hidden Hills]] at a '''25%''' chance and in [[Faerie's Brake]] at a '''12%''' chance.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a grey wolf, but is slightly larger than the younger wolf cubs.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams amongst its pack, protecting the wolf cubs. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or a nearby enemy are [[Lunar Madness|provoked]].

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} is, oddly enough, weaker than the younger wolf cubs. It has less HP and AC than the younger varieties, and shouldn't be an issue for a character of any level to take down.
