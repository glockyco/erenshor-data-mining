[[Weapons]] in [[Erenshor]] let you decide how you deal physical damage.

There are multiple types of weapons with different damage and delay.

Weapons divide into four main categories - Primary which are equipped in the Main Hand, Secondary which are equipped in the Off Hand, Primary - 2-Handed which are equipped in the main hand and take up both hand slots, and Primary or Secondary which can be equipped in either hand.
