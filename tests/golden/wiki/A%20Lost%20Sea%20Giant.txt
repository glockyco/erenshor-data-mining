'''{{main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found on the eastern cape of [[Vitheo's Watch]], near the ruins.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a ghostly blue apparition. {{PAGENAME}} can be male or female, with the female variants being three levels higher than that of the males. There is a '''50%''' chance for each variant to spawn.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the ruins on the coast. It will attack players if they get too close, or if it or a nearby Lost Sea Giant are provoked.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
