''{{Main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found in a hidden building in the northeast of [[Soluna's Landing]]. There is a small gap in the cliffs near the hill with three [[Wild Ogre|Wild Ogres]]. In the subsequent valley, head northwest and into the large back room of the building to find Raval.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} appears as a skeletal figure wearing orange and silver armor. He also wears bracers and boots, and wields a sword.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits behind the table underneath a large picture for players to approach. He will attack if players get to close, or if he or his nearby guards are provoked.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
Being a [[Paladin]], Raval is fairly tanky, although he does not use any of the spells or abilities you'd expect from his class. It's a good idea to take out the [[Ancient Knight|Ancient Knights]] that flank him as quickly as possible, or otherwise keep them stunned using {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}}, {{AbilityLink|Sleep}}, or {{AbilityLink|Sunburst}}, as they have the ability to heal Reval for large amounts of health using their {{AbilityLink|Supreme Healing}}.

=Notes=
*Beating Raval causes the [[Freed Spirit of Raval]] to show up in the cemetery behind the building, where the quest {{QuestLink|Raval's Last Wish}} can be acquired.
