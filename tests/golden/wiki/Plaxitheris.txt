''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found atop a hill on the northern cliffs of [[Stowaway's Step]], past [[Treven Pines|Treven Pines']] shop and through the stone archway.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a [[Mature Malaroth]] that has somehow ended up on Stowaway's Step. It has sharp teeth, two large horns, and spikes protruding from nearly everywhere on its body.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits atop a rock on top of the hill. His detection range is rather large, and he will attack players should they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} has strong physical attacks and can easily take down an underleveled or undergeared tank faster than they can be healed. Make sure your [[Paladin]] is well-equipped before attempting to take on {{PAGENAME}}, or consider backing them up with multiple party members assisting with healing.

=Notes=
*{{PAGENAME}} drops A Moongill, which can be used to complete {{QuestLink|Wally's Moongill}} if the player does not wish to spend time trying to [[Fishing|Fish]] for it.
