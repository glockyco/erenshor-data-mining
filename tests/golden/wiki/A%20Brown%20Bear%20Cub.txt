'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} are common throughout southern [[Erenshor]] and can be found in [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]],<ref>There is a '''1% chance''' for {{PAGENAME}} to be replaced by the [[Sylvan Giant]] in Fernalla's Revival Plains.</ref> [[Duskenlight Coast]],<ref>There is a '''1% chance''' for {{PAGENAME}} to be replaced by the [[Dusken Giant]] in Duskenlight Coast.</ref> [[Faerie's Brake]], and on [[Stowaway's Step]]. {{PAGENAME}} can also be found in a small cave connected to the [[Island Tomb]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a small adolescent bear with brown fur.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a player to attack. Being a cub, it is quite weak.
