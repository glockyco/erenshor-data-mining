Elden is located at the archaeologist dig site in the [[Braxonian Desert]]. He is the quest giver for {{QuestLink|link=The Scent of Brashell}}

Handing him both [[Braxonian Scripture]]s completes {{QuestLink|link=Braxonian Scripture (Quest){{!}}Braxonian Scripture}}

Greeting message:
{| class="wikitable"
|We have reason to believe that the lost people of this place may exist beneath our very feet.
|}
