'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is a common pest and can be found throughout southern Erenshor, including [[Hidden Hills]], [[Vitheo's Watch]], [[Faerie's Brake]]<ref>{{PAGENAME}} often has a chance to spawn instead of another enemy. If this happens, {{PAGENAME}}'s base respawn time will be based on the original enemy.</ref>, and on [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} has black appendages and multiple eyes. Its green body and black legs help it blend in with the grass, hence its name.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a player to attack. They are quite weak, being akin to slimes in many other games.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
