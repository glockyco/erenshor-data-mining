'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}}s are found in the Bandit's Lair on [[Stowaway's Step]]. The cave entrance can be found near [[Treven Pines|Treven Pines']] shop.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}}s are bandits wearing grey and red clothes, shoes, and gloves. They can occasionally be seen holding a [[Brackwood Mace]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}}s roam throughout their lair and will attack players if they get too close. There are several varieties of bandits, though the only one that has the ability to cast [[Ability Books|spells]] is the female bandit with hair.

===Spells & Abilities===
Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Magic Bolt}}
