This note is less direct than others in the quest and requires some extrapolation: the torch at 2 o'clock should be lit white, corresponding with the "snow-like seedlings." As more come next, 3 o'clock should also be white. Finally, the 4'clock torch should be lit blue, hinted at by the color of the sea.
