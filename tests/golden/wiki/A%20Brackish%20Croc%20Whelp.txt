'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found exclusively outside of the lighthouse in [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a small black-scaled crocodile. It is the smallest of all the croc variants.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the path on the way from the registration office to the lighthouse. It will attack players when they approach it. {{PAGENAME}} is the first [[Enemies|enemy]] players will encounter, and they will be prompted with a short tutorial upon approaching it.
