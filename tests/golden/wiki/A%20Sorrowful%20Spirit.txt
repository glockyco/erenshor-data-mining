'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found in the deeper parts of the [[Island Tomb]]<ref>Sorrowful Spirits in the corridors surrounding [[Failed Cremation]] can rarely be replaced by a [[Rotting Mummy]].</ref> on [[Stowaway's Step]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a small floating spirit in brown robes. While it has no head or legs, it does have two skeletal arms and hands extending from its robes.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the tomb and will attack players if they get too close. They are physically weak, preferring to cast spells that both [[Status Effects|buff]] themselves and harm the player's party.
