Faerie Drones are small, magic enemies that spawn in [[Faerie's Brake]]. Due to their small hitbox they prove difficult to target and attack, so it is recommended to use the TAB targeting system. Faerie Drones can drop a [[Bag of Faerie Dust]], required for the quest "[[Faerie Dust for Nylith Valarro]]."
