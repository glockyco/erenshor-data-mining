''{{main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
There is a '''79% chance''' for {{PAGENAME}} to replace [[A Brackish Croc]] in the center of the beach on the coast of [[Vitheo's Watch]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is easily spotted from the top of the cliff leading down to the beach due to his size. Although similar in appearance, he is much larger than the other Brackish Crocs in the area.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the beach and will attack low-level players if they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
Although he only attacks physically, {{PAGENAME}} can easily overpower an underleveled or undergeared character. Players may wish to steer clear until they've explored some of [[Old Krakengard]] and gained some additional experience and gear.

When fighting {{PAGENAME}}, be wary of the surrounding Brackish Crocs to avoid adding additional enemies to the fight.

=Notes=
*It is possible to receive two pieces of the same equipment upon defeating {{PAGENAME}}.
