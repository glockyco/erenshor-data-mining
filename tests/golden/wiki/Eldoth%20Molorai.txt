''{{Main|Enemies}}''
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found on the top floor of the building in the middle of [[Azynthi's Garden]]. His room is hidden behind a bookcase, which requires the {{ItemLink|Intricate Key}} to open. This key is dropped by the [[Molorai Eldermistress]], who can be found on the balcony on the opposite side of the building.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a tall man in finery and a large top hat. He wears brown boots and a red and black cape, which is clasped around his neck with a gold chain. Although it is unconfirmed, it is quite likely that {{PAGENAME}} (and many of his followers) are vampiric in nature, further evidenced by his sharp teeth and innate lifesteal.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} hides in his secret library and will attack players when they enter. As players explore the Garden, {{PAGENAME}} will also shout various messages, encouraging his supporters and taunting the player.

*{{AbilityLink|Blood Feast}}

===Strategy===
Fighting {{PAGENAME}} at full strength is incredibly difficult and not recommended. Instead, players should seek out and destroy the four [[Flicker of Souls]] scattered throughout Azynthi's Garden before challenging him:
*Near the front steps of the castle.
*Inside a mausoleum in the graveyard.
*In a seemingly inaccessible section of the hedge maze.
*Sealed inside a room on the first floor of the castle.
Each of the Flickers are immune to damage until given a {{ItemLink|Riftsnare Gem}}. These can be found by defeating [[Enemies#List of Bosses|Bosses]] in the Dimensional Rift version of Azynthi's Garden, reached by completing {{QuestLink|The Riftstone}} and acquiring the {{ItemLink|Azynthian Riftstone}}:
*[[Grounds Keeper]] in the hedge maze, in front of the statue of [[Sivakaya]].
*[[Vos Ashlyn]] in the graveyard.
*[[Exalted Firebone]], on the second floor of the castle in the middle room.
*[[Oracle Miress]], in the room to the left when facing Exalted Firebone.
*[[Oracle Tretha]], on the balcony on the top floor of the castle.
Each Flicker of Souls destroyed weakens {{PAGENAME}}, up to a maximum of four times. Players will need to work quickly, however, as they will respawn 50 minutes (27 minutes and 30 seconds if in a party of three or four) after they've been destroyed and subsequently grant {{PAGENAME}} his power back.<br><br>

When fighting {{PAGENAME}}, be aware of his innate lifesteal: if your DPS is not high enough to counteract his healing, you will need to [[Item Quality|upgrade]] your gear or gain additional levels before challenging him again. Even after being weakened, {{PAGENAME}} still hits hard, but does not use any special skills or abilities. With enough damage and a tank able to withstand his hits, {{PAGENAME}} will eventually fall.

=Notes=
*Players will need to neutralize the Flickers of Souls each time they challenge {{PAGENAME}} if they wish to weaken him again.
*There is currently a known bug causing {{PAGENAME}} to not grant any Experience or his related Steam Achievement when he is defeated after being fully weakened. This can be bypassed by only killing three of the four [[Flicker of Souls]]. Note that this will, however, cause {{PAGENAME}} to be more difficult.
*{{PAGENAME}} may occasionally sense players through the walls, floor, or ceiling of his room and attack without provocation.
*If {{PAGENAME}} is respawned via a {{ItemLink|Time Stone}}, all [[Flicker of Souls]] will respawn as well and must be destroyed to weaken him again.
