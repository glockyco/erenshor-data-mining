=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found in the deepest part of [[The Bone Pits]] in front of the statue of [[Sivakaya]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a large red demon, with limbs that appear to float in the air independent of each other.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} will not move until it spots a player to attack or is [[Lunar Madness|provoked]]. Upon {{PAGENAME}} targeting a player, the surrounding [[Neophyte Necromancer|Neophyte Necromancers]] will also become aggressive and swarm the party.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.

===Strategy===
An [[Arcanist|Arcanist's]] {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}} can help with crowd control. Additionally, it is possible to preemptively defeat Neophyte Necromancers without drawing the attention of {{PAGENAME}} if they wander far enough away from the altar.

=Notes=
*It is possible to receive two pieces of the same equipment upon defeating {{PAGENAME}}.
