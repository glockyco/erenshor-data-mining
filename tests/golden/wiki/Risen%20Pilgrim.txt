'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is a special variant of [[A Brittle Skeleton]] found throughout [[Silkengrass Meadowlands]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a small skeleton wearing tattered grey pants, a torn blue shirt, and manacles on both wrists.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} is a reanimated corpse with little intelligence or strength. They will roam the area until finding an adventurer to attack, but are hardly dangerous unless encountered in large numbers.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
