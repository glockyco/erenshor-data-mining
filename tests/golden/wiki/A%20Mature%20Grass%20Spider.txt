'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is a common pest and can be found throughout most of northern [[Erenshor]], including [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]], [[Blacksalt Strand]],<ref>{{PAGENAME}} may be replaced by [[A Seaside Gorilla]] or [[A Grizzly Bear]] in Blacksalt Strand.</ref> [[Ripper's Keep]], [[Malaroth's Nesting Grounds]], and [[Azynthi's Garden]]. It can also be found on the fields of [[Vitheo's Watch]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} has black appendages, a green body, and multiple eyes. It is the larger adult version of [[A Grass Spider]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} will roam through grasslands and meadows until it senses a target to attack. Although they are larger than their younger brood, they are still quite weak and shouldn't pose much of a challenge to players by the time they reach the zones {{PAGENAME}} can be found in.

{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
