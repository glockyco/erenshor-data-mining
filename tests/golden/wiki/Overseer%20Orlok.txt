=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a tall man in green and red armor with a banner on his back. He is flanked by two [[Molorai Bodyguard|Molorai Bodyguards]].

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in the tower at the top of the fort. Although he will not attack as soon as players open the door, players should still be cautious when entering the hallway as {{PAGENAME}} and his guards may attack from around the corner.

===Strategy===
This fight is considerably more difficult than that with Sigrid, so players may wish to gain a few more levels before challenging {{PAGENAME}}.<br>

{{PAGENAME}} will continually [[Status Effects|buff]] himself while idle, but will no longer cast these spells once combat begins and they will quickly wear off. While {{PAGENAME}} alone can already overwhelm an underprepared party, his bodyguards can be lethal at lower levels if they are not quickly stunned with {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}} or {{AbilityLink|Sunburst}}.
