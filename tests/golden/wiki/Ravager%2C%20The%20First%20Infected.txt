''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} can be found in a dead-end of the [[Elderstone Mines]] surrounded by several glowing mushrooms. When entering the mines from [[Blacksalt Strand]], following the path down into the caves and turn right, then left. Ravager will be at the end of the path.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a larger version of the [[A Fungus Ravaged Brute|Fungus Ravaged Brutes]], with red and pink pustules covering its body and several gashes sewn together by roots.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams his alcove and will attack players if they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===
Defensive:

Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Minor Lightning}}
*{{AbilityLink|Jolt}}

=Strategy=
{{PAGENAME}} is a relatively straightforward fight, as he has no guards or minions players need to worry about. Ravager uses several offensive spells, though they can be mitigated through resists or interrupted with a well-timed {{AbilityLink|Kick}}.
