''{{main|Enemies}}''

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} has a '''95% chance''' to spawn in the [[Elderstone Mines]] on the path between the cliff overlooking the lake and the dead-end where [[Ravager, The First Infected]] can be found.

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a serpentine enemy wearing bangles on each wrist and a belt. It is larger than the other Deeplings, with dark blue hands and scales. Its belly is white.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} roams the mines and will attack players if they get too close.

===Spells & Abilities===

Offensive:

*{{AbilityLink|Ethereal Rending}}

=Strategy=
{{PAGENAME}} uses a wide variety of offensive spells, so raising your Magic and Elemental Resists can greatly negate its damage. Be wary of adds joining the fight; players can either clear out nearby enemies before engaging {{PAGENAME}}, or else use crowd control like {{AbilityLink|Dazzle}} or {{AbilityLink|Sunburst}} to stun them.
