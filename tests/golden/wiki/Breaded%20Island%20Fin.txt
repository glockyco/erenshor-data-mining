'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[Consumables|Consumable]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is created as part of the quest {{QuestLink|The Mystery of Bellwain Island}} by trading the Baking Oven in the kitchen the correct ingredients, as listed in {{ItemLink|The Recipe|image=Sivakayan Boat Pass.png}}:
*{{ItemLink|Island Fin}}
*{{ItemLink|Bread}}
*{{ItemLink|Brackfruit Juice}}
*{{ItemLink|Coal}}

While the other three items can be found around the house, the Island Fin can be caught exclusively on [[Bellwain Island]]:

=Description=
Crafting {{PAGENAME}} rewards the player with 20 experience; this can be repeated infinitely.

After creating {{PAGENAME}}, it can be delivered to the picnic blanket near the large tree at the back of the house. Upon doing so, {{ItemLink|Mid Day on the Isle|image=Drafty Baths.png}} will appear.

{{PAGENAME}} can also be consumed to activate {{AbilityLink|Nourished}}.
