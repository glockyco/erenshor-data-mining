=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a faceless floating specter in brown robes and a brown belt. Although he has no feet, legs, or face, skeletal hands still stick out from the robe. He is similar in appearance to the [[A Sorrowful Spirit|Sorrowful Spirits]] that also occupy the tomb, though slightly larger.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in a back storage room of the tomb. He will attack players if they get too close, or if he or the nearby [[An Enraged Spirit|Enraged Spirit]] are attacked or [[Lunar Madness|provoked]].

===Spells & Abilities===
Buffs:
*{{AbilityLink|Hardened Skin}}

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} will likely be one of the first bosses players encounter. As such, he does not have any particularly noteworthy skills, though he will buff himself to absorb a modest amount of damage. If players are struggling to defeat him, they may want to form a [[Simulated Players|party]] or consider gaining a few levels through weaker enemies or by completing [[Quests]] in Stowaway's Step.
