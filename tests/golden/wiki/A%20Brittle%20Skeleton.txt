'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is an [[Enemies|enemy]] in [[Erenshor]].
=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} are common throughout southern [[Erenshor]] and can be found in [[Hidden Hills]], [[Faerie's Brake]], on [[Stowaway's Step]], and in [[The Bone Pits]]. {{PAGENAME}} can also be rarely found in the [[Island Tomb]] as well as the [[Braxonian Desert]].

=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a small skeleton wearing tattered grey pants, a torn blue shirt, and manacles on both wrists.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} is a reanimated corpse with little intelligence or strength. They will roam the area until they find an adventurer to attack, but are far from dangerous unless encountered in large numbers.

===Spells & Abilities===
{{PAGENAME}} does not use any spells or abilities.
