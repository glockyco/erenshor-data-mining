=Appearance=
{{PAGENAME}} is a glowing blue spirit wearing tattered brown clothes. There is a red glow from under his hood and mask, where only two white eyes are visible.

=Behavior=
{{PAGENAME}} waits in a back storage room of the tomb. Although he will not attack players the moment they open the door, his detection range is quite large and he may be notice the player's party even before they've entered the room.

===Spells & Abilities===
Offensive:
*{{AbilityLink|Magic Bolt}}
*{{AbilityLink|Minor Lightning}}
*{{AbilityLink|Tangle}}

===Strategy===
{{PAGENAME}} will likely be the final boss players encounter before leaving [[Stowaway's Step]]. Although his health is not very high, his AC is a step up from anything players have fought before, making him slightly more difficult to hit. He has several spells in his arsenal, including Minor Lighting. This is an AoE spell that can damage the entire party, though it is fairly weak. A [[Paladin]] or [[Duelist]] can interrupt {{PAGENAME}} while he is casting with a well-timed {{AbilityLink|Kick}}.
