'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[Teleportation|Teleportation Item]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is the reward given for completing {{QuestLink|link=The Mystery of Bellwain Island}}. It can be found in Bellwain's Master Bedroom after obtaining {{ItemLink|Bellwain's Master Key}}.

=Description=
{{PAGENAME}} teleports the user back to the last [[Wishing Well]] the player saved at. It can either be right clicked to use it from the inventory, or it can be placed into a hotkey slot and used by pressing the corresponding key.
