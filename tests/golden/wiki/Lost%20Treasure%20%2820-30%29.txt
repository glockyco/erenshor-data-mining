=Location=
Giving the four pieces of a {{ItemLink|Torn Treasure Map (Top Right)|image=Torn Treasure Map.png|text=Torn Treasure Map}} to [[Cecil Threbb]] rewards players with a {{ItemLink|Treasure Map}}, allowing them to go on a [[Treasure Hunting|Treasure Hunt]]. Using this map between levels '''20 - 30''' will reveal the location of a Lost Treasure in one of the following locations:
*[[Hidden Hills]]
*[[Faerie's Brake]]
*[[Fernalla's Revival Plains]]
*[[Silkengrass Meadowlands]]
*[[Duskenlight Coast]]
*[[Soluna's Landing]]

=Appearance=
The location where the Lost Treasure is buried appears as a pile of leaves and dirt with a golden aura.

=Behavior=
Interacting with the dirt will reveal a chest. Attacking the chest will spawn a wave of enemies which scale with the player's level. There are '''2 to 5 waves''' in total. They will consist of any combination of '''3 to 5''' of the following enemies:
*[[Ancient Skeleton]]
*[[Ancient Demon]]
*[[Ancient Horror]]
Once the final wave has been defeated, attacking the chest once more will open it, allowing you to loot the contents inside.
