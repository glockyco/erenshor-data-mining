There are 4 Watchlight's in Stowaway's Step. They are used in a binding spell to keep [[Aragath Swellsteer]] imprisoned. The player can choose to help [[The Necromancers of the Island Tomb]] to finish off Aragath ({{QuestLink|link=Destroying Aragath}}) or choose to help Aragath by using the necromancers soul stones on the watchlight's to sever their grasp ({{QuestLink|link=Aragath's Imprisonment}}).
