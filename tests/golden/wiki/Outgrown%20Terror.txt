Outgrown Terror can be found on the tail of the Gargoyle statue in the [[Shivering Step]]. The Gargoyle statue can be found where the Statue of Vitheo is located in [[Stowaway's Step]]

The Outgrown Terror will give the player a [[Shivering Step Lighthouse Key]] if the player says the name 'Sned' to it.
