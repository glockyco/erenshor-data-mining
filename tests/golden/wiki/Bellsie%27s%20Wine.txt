'''{{PAGENAME}}''' is a [[Quest Items|Quest Item]] in [[Erenshor]].

=Location=
{{PAGENAME}} is found in a cavern beneath the docks during {{QuestLink|link=The Mystery of Bellwain Island}}. Its location is hinted at by the note {{ItemLink|The Stash|image=Marching Orders.png}}.

=Description=
{{PAGENAME}} can be delivered to the empty seat near the campfire behind the pergola, as instructed by the note {{ItemLink|Relaxation|image=Marching Orders.png}}. Upon doing so, {{ItemLink|Afternoon on the Isle|image=Drafty Baths.png}} will appear.
