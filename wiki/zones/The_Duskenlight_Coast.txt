The Duskenlight Coast is located in the west of [[Erenshor]]. It is connected to the [[Hidden Hills]] and connects to the [[Windwashed Pass]].

The entrance to the dungeon [[Jaws of Sivakaya]] lies within the giant skull which can be prominently seen throughout the zone.
