=== Unsealing the Jaws ===
Unlike most dungeons, some prerequisites must be met in order unseal the dungeon to venture further inside.<br><br>Prerequisites:
#Complete the quest {{QuestLink|A Hermit's Request}} in [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]] to obtain the [[Notes of a Hermit]] 
#Read the [[Notes of a Hermit]] to obtain the quest {{QuestLink|A Note for C. Pillot}} . Hand them to [[Chazz Pillot]] to obtain [[Mold: A Strange Figurine]].
<br><br>
Unlocking steps:
#Complete {{QuestLink|Recovering the Nighthollow Candle}} and {{QuestLink|Lighting the Nighthollow Candle}} . This questline is started in Loomingwood.
#Having the [[Nighthollow Candle]] in your inventory will cause [[A Memory of Sivakaya]] to appear in the entrance area. Speaking to the memory completes the quest {{QuestLink|The Guardian Awakens}} .
#In the next chamber, the statue of Sivakaya will begin to rotate. Whilst facing the statue, strafe around the room meeting it's gaze (it's eyes will glow). If successful, the quest {{QuestLink|She Sees You}} will complete and hidden door will open granting access to the rest of the dungeon.
#To proceed past [[Aeiros, Soldier of Sivakaya]], players will need to complete {{QuestLink|A Pious Guest}} by saying <span style="color: #15e300;">[Azynthi]</span> to the [[Ward of Piety]]
