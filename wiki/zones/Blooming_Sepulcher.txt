Blooming Sepulcher is a multiple area dungeon located beneath the willow in [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]]. It is tied heavily to [[Vitheo's Rest]] and players will need to progress in both zones to delve deeper into the Sepulcher's mysteries.

==Upper Dungeon==
Upon entering the dungeon for the first time, players will encounter [[A Protected Memory]]. Killing this entity will cause [[A Dying Wish]] to spawn, which will provide the quest {{QuestLink|link=A Dying Wish (Quest)#Part_One{{!}}A Dying Wish, Pt. 1}}. Completing this step by killing [[The Crypt Guardian]] in [[Vitheo's Rest]] and turning in the {{ItemLink|Memory of a Guardian}} to the dying memory will remove the barrier and allow access to the area.

Bringing a {{ItemLink|Vithean Brick}} to the [[Remnant]] near the [[Fernalla's Revival Plains]] entrance will open a portal to [[Vitheo's Rest]] and allow faster travel between the dungeons.

==The Pit==
Further into the upper part of the dungeon, the player will find another [[A Protected Memory|Protected Memory]], which will spawn another [[A Dying Wish|Dying Wish]] and provide {{QuestLink|link=A Dying Wish (Quest)#Part_Two{{!}}A Dying Wish, Pt. 2}}. This will require killing [[Balaste the Fallen]] in the first wing of [[Vitheo's Rest]] and retrieving the {{ItemLink|Memory of Balaste}}.

==The Willow==
Entrance to the Willow will require killing a third [[A Protected Memory|Protected Memory]] and getting {{QuestLink|link=A Dying Wish (Quest)#Part_Three{{!}}A Dying Wish, Pt. 3}} from another [[A Dying Wish|Dying Wish]]. Beware that this Protected Memory spawns [[Elder Guardian|Elder Guardians]] at set intervals during the fight. This one requires killing [[Elwio the Traitor]] in [[Vitheo's Rest]] and bringing the {{ItemLink|Memory of Elwio}} back.

Note: It is possible to jump off the ledge above the lake area and the [[Simulated Players]] will follow, saving the trip of running around the long way. However, this will aggro several mobs, which can be deadly if the player is not prepared.
