Vitheo's Rest is a multi level dungeon and the first you encounter. It contains three separate levels locked off by keys. Vitheo's Rest is located in [[Port Azure]] behind a hidden wall near the docks.

To not get attacked by the Aquilamar you must say the passcode 'Shadowtide' to [[Styria Olafur]]

To gain access to Vitheo's Rest you must either give [[Vethalo Gala]] a {{ItemLink|Sivakrux}} or alternatively you can kill [[Aareth Graveblade]] for a spare key.

Access to the Shrines and Traitor's Halls requires completion of {{QuestLink|link=A Dying Wish (Quest)#Part_One{{!}}A Dying Wish, Pt. 1}} and {{QuestLink|link=A Dying Wish (Quest)#Part_Two{{!}}A Dying Wish, Pt. 2}}, respectively, and is progressed alongside unlocking new sections of the [[Blooming Sepulcher]] dungeon. Access to Vitheo's Tomb requires acquiring the {{ItemLink|Key to Vitheo's Tomb}} from [[The Abomination]] in the Traitor's Halls. 

Spawning [[The Abomination]] in the Traitor's Halls requires completing {{QuestLink|The Life Flame}} and {{QuestLink|The Dark Flame}}, which in turn require acquiring the quest {{QuestLink|link=A Dying Wish (Quest)#Part_Four{{!}}A Dying Wish, Pt. 4}} in the [[Blooming Sepulcher]] dungeon. 

The Under the Lighthouse section is accessed from a separate entrance, the Port Azure Lighthouse, after completion of {{QuestLink|Sivakayans in Port Azure}}.
